The sovereign state of Cambodia has a population of over 17 million.[12] Buddhism is enshrined in the constitution as the official state religion, and is practised by more than 97% of the population.[13] Cambodia's minority groups include Vietnamese, Chinese, Chams and 30 hill tribes.[14] Cambodia has a tropical monsoon climate of two seasons, and the country is made up of a central floodplain around the Tonlé Sap lake and Mekong Delta, surrounded by mountainous regions.
